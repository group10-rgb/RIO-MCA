Meet Coach Rio

Wilfread Kiarie Mwaura, known affectionately as Coach Rio, is a community leader, mentor, and champion for the people of Nairobi South.

With years of grassroots experience and a proven track record of bringing people together, Coach Rio understands the challenges facing our community and has the vision to transform them into opportunities.

His leadership is built on integrity, accountability, and a genuine commitment to serve the people of Nairobi South with excellence.
